Our Success

And Company History.

H S Media & Films Pvt Ltd is a premier Camera Equipment Rental Company located in the vibrant hub of Goregaon West, Mumbai. Established in 2013, we have emerged as a trusted name in the industry, offering an extensive array of cutting-edge camera gear, including DSLRs, Professional Camcorders, 4K cameras, and top-notch Sound Setups available for hire to cater to a diverse range of shooting requirements.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond merely offering state-of-the-art equipment; we strive to deliver personalized service and support to ensure that your filming experience is seamless and successful. With our dedicated team of professionals and a meticulous attention to detail, we aim to be your trusted partner in bringing your creative vision to life.

Choose H S Media & Films Pvt Ltd for all your camera equipment rental needs, and experience the difference quality makes in your productions. Contact us today to discuss your project requirements and let us tailor a solution that exceeds your expectations.
